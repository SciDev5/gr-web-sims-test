
Christoffel symbols if $g_{\mu\nu}$ is diagonal,
$
\Gamma ^\lambda _{\mu\nu} = \frac{1}{2 g _{\lambda\lambda}} ( g_{\mu\lambda,\nu} + g_{\nu\lambda,\mu} - g_{\mu\nu,\lambda} )$

Geodesic equation:
$
\frac{\partial^2 p^\lambda}{\partial \tau^2} = -\Gamma ^\lambda _{\mu\nu} \frac{\partial p^\mu}{\partial \tau} \frac{\partial p^\nu}{\partial \tau} $

Geodesic equation (dot notation):
$
\ddot p^\lambda = -\Gamma ^\lambda _{\mu\nu} \.p^\mu \.p^\nu $
